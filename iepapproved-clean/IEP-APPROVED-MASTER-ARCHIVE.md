## The Know Me Method
**Proprietary advocacy framework by Kimberly Sandro**
- Pre-meeting email with child's photos and personality (1-2 days before)
- Open IEP meeting by introducing the child as a person first (3-5 min)
- Continuous reframing throughout using humor and personal stories
- Close every meeting with the child
- Tagline: "Because every child deserves to be known before they are evaluated."
